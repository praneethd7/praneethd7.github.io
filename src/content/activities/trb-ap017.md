**Active Member**

Serving as an appointed member of the standing technical committee [AP017](https://www.nationalacademies.org/committees/AP017) under the Transportation Research Board (TRB), a division of the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine.

- Participate in identifying research needs and sharing critical findings related to transit data.
- Contribute to reviewing research papers, evaluating proposals, and fostering dialogue on emerging transportation technologies.
