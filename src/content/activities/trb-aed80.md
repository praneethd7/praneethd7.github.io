**Aug 2024 - Jun 2025**

As the Communications Coordinator for the AED80 Committee under the Transportation Research Board (TRB), I managed our digital presence and facilitated information sharing within our transportation visualization community.

Key responsibilities included:
- **Digital Presence**: Maintaining and updating the committee website to ensure accurate and timely information.
- **Newsletter Curation**: Curating and distributing the quarterly *Visualization Roundup* newsletter.
- **Outreach & Communication**: Supporting various committee communication initiatives and updates.
- **Subcommittee Participation**: Actively participating in specialized subcommittees (including *BIM for Infrastructure*, *Performance Visualization*, *Interactive and Immersive Simulation*, and *Cartographic Visualization*).

This volunteer role allowed me to contribute to the advancement of visualization techniques in the transportation sector.
